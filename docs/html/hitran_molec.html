List of Molecules

  The following table lists the molecules presently in the line-by-line, traditional part of the HITRAN database, showing their assigned number in the HITRAN sequence along with their isotopologues (using the AFGL shorthand notation).
  Listed are the spectral extent and the number of transitions for each isotopologue. For comparison, the corresponding values from the previous HITRAN2008 database are also given in the table.

  The abundance values are based on P. De Bievre, N.E. Holden, and I.L. Barnes, “Isotopic Abundances and Atomic Weights of the Elements,” J.Phys.Chem.Ref.Data 13, 809-891 (1984).
